02 — Brand Story

Why TobSar Exists

Cambodian social commerce . But sellers answer every inquiry by hand — 18 hours a day, 7 days a week. TobSar changes that.

The Origin

Every Cambodian seller is trapped in their own inbox.

They built real businesses but success became the bottleneck. The more customers message, the more hours they spend copy-pasting prices, checking stock, and confirming payments. Manually. By hand. Every. Single. Message.

TobSar was built to give them their life back — an AI that speaks Khmer, knows their products, and closes orders while they sleep.

Clear Space Rule

Maintain minimum clear space equal to the icon's corner radius (x) on all four sides. No text, logos, photos, or UI elements inside this exclusion zone.

At ≤ 32px icon size, increase clear space to 1.5x minimum. At ≤ 16px, use text-only "TobSar" wordmark.

What Not to Do

🚫

Don't recolor the icon

Logo stays #0098FE on light, white on dark. No other fill colors permitted.

🚫

Don't stretch or skew

Always maintain original 1:1 aspect ratio. Scale proportionally only.

🚫

Don't add effects

No drop shadows, glows, gradients, or filters directly on the mark.

🚫

Don't use on busy BGs

Only use on solid, low-pattern backgrounds. Choose dark or white variant.

🚫

Don't use below 24px

Icon loses legibility below 24px. Use plain "TobSar" text instead.

🚫

Don't create new variants

Only the six variants shown above are approved for any use.

10 — Motion System

Motion System

Motion communicates state, guides attention, and adds personality. Every animation has a purpose — never decorative. TobSar's motion is fast, smooth, and purposeful.

⚡

Purposeful

Every transition earns its place. If removing it doesn't hurt comprehension, remove it. Never animate for decoration.

🎯

Responsive

UI responds to input instantly (≤100ms). Feedback latency above 200ms feels broken to users in a chat-speed app.

🌊

Natural

Use spring easing for interactive elements. Ease-decelerate for things entering. Ease-accelerate for things leaving.

Duration Scale

--dur-fast · 100ms

Button hover, toggle switch, badge state change. Anything that needs to feel instant.

--dur-normal · 200ms

Dropdown open/close, tooltip appear, input focus ring. The default for most UI transitions.

--dur-slow · 400ms

Page transitions, modal open, bottom sheet slide up. Used when something significant enters the screen.

--dur-deliberate · 600ms

Onboarding celebrations, success confetti, hero entrance on first load. Deliberate, special moments.

Easing Curves

⟳

Standard

cubic-bezier(0.4,0,0.2,1)

General UI transitions. The default for most cases.

↘

Decelerate

cubic-bezier(0,0,0.2,1)

Things entering the screen — modals, toasts, panels sliding in.

↗

Accelerate

cubic-bezier(0.4,0,1,1)

Things leaving the screen — dismissals, elements fading out.

🌱

Spring

cubic-bezier(0.34,1.56,0.64,1)

Interactive elements — toggles, buttons on press, playful micro-interactions.

.btn-primary { transition: background var(--dur-fast) var(--ease-standard); }

/* ── Modal entering — slow + decelerate ── */

/* ── Chat message appearing ── */

/* ── Toggle switch — spring makes it feel physical ── */

11 — Illustration Style

Illustration Style

TobSar uses a minimal, single-color icon system and simple geometric spot illustrations. The style is clean, modern, and works equally well in light and dark modes.

Rounded Corners

All icons use round stroke caps and joins. Corner radius = 20% of icon size.

2px Stroke

12 Writing Rules

01

Lead with outcomes, not features. "You made $125 while sleeping" beats "AI auto-reply enabled."

02

Use present tense. "TobSar handles your inbox" not "TobSar will handle your inbox."

03

Use specific numbers. "<5 seconds" not "fast." "134 orders" not "many orders."

04

Write short sentences. Maximum 20 words per sentence. If it has two ideas — split it.

05

Name the real person. "Like Sreynea — a TikTok seller" not "users like you."

06

One idea per sentence. No compound ideas separated by commas. No "and also."

07

Never use passive voice. "TobSar replied" not "the reply was sent by TobSar."

08

Khmer first in Cambodia. Always lead the Khmer version of a sentence, then English below it.

09

No tech jargon in UI. "AI is thinking…" not "Processing NLP inference pipeline."

10

Celebrate outcomes directly. "You just made $125" not "Your sales metrics show improvement."

11

Errors are calm, not alarming. "Something went wrong. Try again." Never red text walls of technical detail.

12

CTAs start with action verbs. "Track order" not "Order tracking." "Set up AI" not "AI setup."

14.3 — Khmernglish

The language TobSar was built to speak

Standard AI fails in Cambodia's DMs because they try to process pure Khmer or pure English. Real commerce conversations are fluid, mixed, slang-heavy — and TobSar was trained on them.

The AI mirrors the customer's language mix. If they write Khmernglish, it responds in Khmernglish. If they write formal Khmer, it responds formally. It never forces code-switching on the customer.
